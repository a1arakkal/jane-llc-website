Professional Research Assistant — University of Colorado Denver, Anschutz Medical Campus (April 2018 – May 2019)

Managed study data and applied advanced statistical techniques to analyze injury patterns, including traumatic brain injuries, concussions, and musculoskeletal injuries. Developed custom SAS macros to automate data processing, enhance reproducibility, and facilitate collaboration with researchers. Co-authored multiple peer-reviewed publications on sports injury epidemiology and the effectiveness of injury prevention strategies.
